# SEO Outreach Master Prompt

**Use Case:** Securing high-authority backlinks through guest posting.

**The Prompt:**
"Act as a professional Link Building Outreach Specialist for [Website Name]. Our goal is to secure a guest post on [Target Website URL]. 
1. Identify the target audience's primary pain point related to [Topic].
2. Create a personalized outreach email (under 150 words) that offers value rather than just asking for a link.
3. Suggest a compelling blog post title that is both SEO-friendly and click-worthy.
4. Tone: Professional, authoritative, and non-spammy."
